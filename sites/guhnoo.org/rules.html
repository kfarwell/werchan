Rules

By using this website, you agree that you'll follow these rules:

Global

  1. Do not post anything that violates local, Canadian, or United States law.
  2. Don't post lewd things outside /lewd/.

Board-specific

Computer-related stuff

/ct/

Content should pertain to consumer electronics and their software such as personal computers, smartphones, cameras, digital audio players, and GPS systems. Most hardware goes here.

  • Free Software, Open Hardware and RYF hardware go on /free/.
  • Operating systems go on /os/.
  • Customization and hacking go on /nasa/.

/free/

Content should pertain to Free Software, hardware that runs Free Software, and Open Hardware. Reverse-engineering non-free software and breaking DRM is welcome too.

  • Gratis/no-cost software goes on /ct/.
  • Non-technical aspects of Android go on /ct/.
  • Technical aspects of Android go on /os/.
  • Technical aspects of writing and modifying Free Software go on /prog/.
  • Hacking on werchan goes on /meta/.
  • GNU/Linux goes on /os/.
  • Non-software politics go on /pol/.
  • Completely free operating systems (FSDG distros, Replicant) stay on /free/.
  • Hardware and software that are not completely free go on /ct/ unless content pertains to freeing them.
  • Content about freedom in regards to any of the above stays on /free/.

/nasa/

Content should pertain to hardware hacking or customizing software such as window managers, music players, browsers, and shells. Wallpaper threads also go here.

  • Non-technical aspects of modifying software go on /free/.
  • Technical aspects of modifying software go on /prog/.

/os/

Content should pertain to operating systems including GNU/Linux, *BSD, OS X, Windows, and Plan 9.

  • Non-technical aspects of writing and modifying operating systems go on /free/.
  • Technical aspects of writing and modifying operating systems go on /prog/.
  • Politics and licensing go on /free/.
  • Completely free operating systems (FSDG distros, Replicant) go on /free/.
  • Non-technical aspects of Windows, OS X, Android, and iOS go on /ct/.

/prog/

Content should pertain to programming, including projects, languages, and editors.

  • Politics and licensing go on /free/.
  • Operating systems go on /os/.
  • Hacking on werchan goes on /meta/.

Other

/art/

Content should pertain to drawing, 3d graphics, music, papercraft, and other forms or art.

  • Programming goes on /prog/.
  • Hacking goes on /nasa/.
  • Video games go on /vidya/.
  • Anime and manga go on /weeb/.
  • Art software stays on /art/.
  • Discussion of the freeness of art software goes on /free/.

/int/

Content should pertain to foreign culture and language.

  • Politics go on /pol/.

/lewd/

Content should be lewd.

/misc/

Anything that follows the global rules but does not fit any other board is allowed.

/pol/

Content should pertain to politics.

  • Software politics go on /free/.

/vidya/

Content should pertain to video games, video game consoles, and gaming PCs.

  • Video game development goes on /prog/.
  • Video games which are Free Software (including development) go on /free/.
  • Steam OS goes on /os/.

/weeb/

Content should pertain to Japanese culture, including anime, manga, and food.

  • Video games go on /vidya/.
  • Wallpapers go on /nasa/.

Meta

/meta/

Content should pertain to werchan itself, including complaints, bug reports, and suggestions.
