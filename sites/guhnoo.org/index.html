What is werchan?

werchan is a unique message board where anyone can share and discuss things based on common interests. Posts can be written in Markdown and HTML, enabling users to easily share a variety of content. Users are anonymous and do not need to register an account before participating in the community. Feel free to click on a board below that interests you and jump right in!

Be sure to familiarize yourself with the rules before posting, and read the FQA if you wish to learn more about how to use the site. You may also wish to read the privacy policy or change your settings.
